Project 2 - MIT AI Watson Lab Assistant

This project was conducted as part of my role as a Research Assistant under Dr. Jie Chen at the MIT AI Watson Lab. My focus was on the efficient implementation of Low-Rank Approximation (LORA) techniques in machine learning.

In this role, I was responsible for developing and optimizing algorithms to reduce computational complexity while maintaining accuracy, allowing faster data processing and enhancing machine learning model efficiency.
